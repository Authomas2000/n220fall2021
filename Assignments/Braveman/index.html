Before you start

1: Click spawn enemy to start a fight

2: Click attack to attack(your attack is first, the enemies is next)

3: Damage is randomized (Random damage + damage - armor)

4: Retreating loses all gold your character has, so be mindful of this!

5: The shop can only be accessed when there is not an enemy and after you faced your first enemy

6: Leveling up increses max health and heals you up to max

7: If the enemies name is red then it is a hard enemy

8: There is a boss every five levels

9: Lastly select a difficuly below to start your game
